Completing Sentences

1. Honesty is the best policy. If you maintain honesty, you can lead a happy life .

2. Patriotism is a noble virtue. It is high time we tried to be patriots .

3. English is an international language. I wish I could speak English fluently .

4. If I had much money, I would help the poor .

5. There goes a proverb that a man is known by the company he keeps . So, you have to make friendship with a gentleman.

6. Hardly has he seen his friend when he greets him .

7. It is a long time since we met each other last .
